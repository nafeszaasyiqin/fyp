FinBuddy

FinBuddy is a recommendation system that allows you to explore various financial aid opportunities for your studies. By inputting specific criteria, such as your study level, family income, major of study, field of study, identity, state, and CGPA. FinAidDiscover will provide tailored recommendations for available financial aid options that match you.

Study Level

Select your study level from the options provided, including Foundation, Diploma, Degree, Master, or PhD.

Family Income

Select your family income range to determine your eligibility for financial aid. Choose from B40 (Less than RM4,850), M40 (RM4,850 - RM10,959), or T20 (RM10,960 and above).

Major of Study

Choose your major of study from available options such as Science & Technology, Social Science & Humanities, or Business & Management.

Field of Study

Based on your chosen major of study, select the specific field of study that aligns with your interests and aspirations.

Identity

Specify your identity, whether it's All, OKU (People with Disabilities), or Orang Asli (Indigenous People), to explore relevant financial aid options.

State

Select your state of residence to find financial aid opportunities specific to your location.

CGPA

Enter your Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA) within the range of 0 to 4 to determine eligibility for certain financial aid programs.
